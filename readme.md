## 🎯 The Portfolio Philosophy: Curriculum from Scratch

Rather than presenting static, pre-defined courses, this repository is organized into discrete **Learning Modules**. This modular design demonstrates my capacity to develop curricula from the ground up—designing content, case studies, workshops, and dynamics with proven success and high student satisfaction.

This methodology has allowed me to collaborate with consulting firms for high-stakes corporate training within Spain’s financial sector:
